Janata Higher Secondary School

Janata Model Higher secondary School (JVT), Tansen Palpa is regarded as one of the finest public school that imparts quality education to achieve academic excellence. It has been able to produce ideal man powers required for our nation and its overall development. This school is still known by its original name JVT even though its looks have changed in the course of time. This school was established with the view of imparting the education of national standard on 21th Bhadra 2008 as the result of that initiation undertaken by Late founder Principal Jagat Bahadur Joshi, Chairman Kebal Prasad Simangaida and their co-workers; It was not an easy task to start a school in such period when the literacy rate in Nepal was under 2 percentage.
